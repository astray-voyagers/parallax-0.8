Welcome To Beyond the Controllers

Why?

The reason to create this website is in order to deliver more streamline information regarding Video games to Professional Gamers or Newbies alike. This website will present the history of video games and how games were like in the past compared to nowadays. Apart from the interesting history of video games, this website will also present fascinating works that many big or small game studios did up till now. Other than that, this website will also include the particulars of video game genres and a few examples to provide insights to how the game genres differ from one another.

About


History

The first portion of this website will tell the tale of how video games came to be like it is in present day. Starting from the first basic 2d graphic game, the classics up until the current generations 3D graphics game with textures that almost looks realistic, how advancement in technologies help further improvements in video games.

Studios

This section will introduce the many competing game studios, the games that their created and a simple summarization of their own history and how they came to be the studios that existed today.

Genre

This segment will introduce the many video game genres today: Action, Strategy, Turn-based, Visual Novel, First Person Shooter and Horror just to name a few. We will try to limit the number of genres that we will cover as there a so many genres of video games out in the market.
